I am working on the “Eigenvalue Problem Involving Non-self Adjoint Differential Operators for the Optical Bent Waveguides” with Prof. Kirankumar R. Hiremath in the multidisciplinary area of optics-photonics. My research study is based on the behavior of optical wave propagation in the waveguides. We(I and my supervisor) use mathematical modeling to tackle the problem analytically by building an eigenvalue problem. Why do an analytical study? Because an analytical study gives further insights into the functioning of the physical system. The operator that corresponds to this eigenvalue problem is a non-self-adjoint operator. Furthermore, we observe that different non-self-adjoint operators behave differently, and their behavior can alter substantially. In this regard, my work will contribute to the evolving field of the general theory of the non-self-adjoint operator. I am eager to discuss anything on non-self-adjoint operators. I am also doing collaborative work with Dr. Segio Manzetti on the Spectral theory of Non-self-adjoint operators and with Dr. Arpit Arvind Khandelwal on Ring resonators.
